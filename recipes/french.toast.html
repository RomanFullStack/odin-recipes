French Toast Recipe

Description

Fresh, tasty, irresistable. This homemade french toast will impress all of your friends for breakfast, and we can make it together with this easy recipe!

Things you'll need

  • 6 slices white bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.
